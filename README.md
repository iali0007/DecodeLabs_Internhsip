# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on E-Commerce Sales Dataset

## Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a cleaned and formatted e-commerce sales dataset. The objective is to uncover meaningful insights, identify trends, detect outliers, and understand customer purchasing behavior through descriptive statistics and data visualization.

The analysis was conducted as part of the **DecodeLabs Data Analytics Industrial Training Program (Project 2: Exploratory Data Analysis)**.

---

## Objectives

- Calculate descriptive statistics (mean, median, count, minimum, maximum)
- Analyze product performance and revenue contribution
- Identify sales trends over time
- Examine customer payment preferences
- Evaluate marketing/referral channel effectiveness
- Detect outliers and unusual transactions
- Generate actionable business insights

---

The dataset contains:

- 1,200 sales transactions

## Exploratory Data Analysis

### Descriptive Statistics

The following statistics were calculated:

- Mean
- Median
- Count
- Minimum
- Maximum

For:

- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Total Price
- Items in Cart

---

### Product Performance Analysis

Products were analyzed based on:

- Number of Orders
- Revenue Generated
- Customer Demand

#### Key Findings

- Chair generated the highest revenue.
- Printer recorded the highest order count.
- Phone generated the lowest revenue.

---

### Order Status Analysis

Order statuses were examined to understand fulfillment performance.

Categories included:

- Delivered
- Shipped
- Pending
- Returned
- Cancelled

#### Key Finding

A significant proportion of orders were either returned or cancelled, indicating potential operational improvement opportunities.

---

### Payment Method Analysis

Customer payment preferences were analyzed across:

- Online Payment
- Credit Card
- Debit Card
- Cash
- Gift Card

#### Key Finding

Online payments emerged as the most preferred payment method.

---

### Referral Source Analysis

Customer acquisition channels included:

- Instagram
- Google
- Email
- Facebook
- Referral

#### Key Finding

Instagram was identified as the most effective customer acquisition channel.

---

### Trend Analysis

Revenue was analyzed over multiple years to identify business performance trends.

#### Key Finding

Revenue and average order value showed a gradual decline from 2023 to 2025.

---

### Outlier Detection

Outliers were detected using statistical techniques to identify unusually high-value transactions.

#### Key Finding

Only a small number of transactions were classified as outliers, indicating a relatively stable dataset.

## Key Insights

- Product revenue is distributed fairly evenly across categories.
- Chairs and Printers contribute the highest revenue.
- Instagram drives the largest number of customer acquisitions.
- Online payments dominate customer transactions.
- Revenue shows a declining trend over the analyzed period.
- High cancellation and return rates may impact profitability.
- Customer purchasing behavior appears stable with minimal extreme outliers.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Investigate causes of high return and cancellation rates.
2. Strengthen marketing efforts on Instagram.
3. Improve customer retention strategies.
4. Increase average order value through product bundling and upselling.
5. Monitor declining revenue trends and adjust sales strategies accordingly.
